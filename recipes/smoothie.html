Yellow Smoothie

Description

My favorite vegan smoothie that I make! Stole this recipe from an old coworker who started his own smoothie truck business. It's super refreshing and tasty. Probably not that healthy for you considering there's probs a lot of sugar but oh well don't care it's dummy fye. Ingredients are usually pretty cheap depending on where you find them. I recommend getting your frozen fruit in bulk and not from those expensive and tiny bags from Whole Foods smh. Follow the measurements exactly!!!

Ingredients

For 2 servings

  • 12 oz of Orange Juice
  • 4 oz of Coconut Milk
  • 1 Cup of Frozen Pineapple
  • 1 Cup of Frozen Mango
  • 1/2 Cup of Dairy-free Yogurt (highly recommend So Delicious' Vanilla Coconut Yogurt!)

Steps

  1. It's a smoothie...just throw all the ingredients into a blender and hit the blend button.
  2. Enjoy!
